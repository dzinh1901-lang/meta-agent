# Chief of Staff Certification

Certification date: 2026-06-20

## Portfolio OS V1.1 Integration Evidence

The Chief of Staff runtime is certified as the portfolio intelligence and governance layer for Portfolio OS V1.1 dry-run operation.

## Portfolio Review

The Chief of Staff layer reviews portfolio registry state, project status, readiness, blockers, pilot evidence, and certification docs.

## Priority Engine

Priority recommendations are certified for dry-run planning and executive briefing. They do not authorize execution.

## Blocker Register

Blockers are recorded for live execution, deployments, secrets, billing, database writes, production writes, external communications, autonomous approvals, and missing owner evidence.

## Owner Action Queue

Owner action queues prepare approval packets and evidence requests. They do not self-approve.

## Executive Brief System

Executive briefs summarize status, risks, pilots, blockers, recommendations, and next milestones.

## Verdict

GO: portfolio review, priority recommendations, blocker analysis, owner action queue, executive briefing.

NO-GO: autonomous approvals, live execution, production writes, deployments, secrets, billing, external communications.
